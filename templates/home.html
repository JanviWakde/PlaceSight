A Placement & Salary Prediction System is designed to assist individuals, particularly students and job seekers, in estimating their chances of securing a job as well as predicting potential salary outcomes. This system leverages various data points such as academic performance, technical and soft skills, prior experience, and industry trends, along with predictive algorithms, to generate valuable insights into a candidate’s career prospects. By analyzing these factors, the system not only evaluates the likelihood of placement opportunities but also provides an estimated salary range. This empowers users to make informed decisions, identify areas for improvement, and prepare more effectively for the competitive job market.
